Poker Preflop Quiz

Scenario 1: AQs vs UTG Raise

Hero holds AQs in HJ. UTG opens 2.5x.

A) Fold
B) 3-bet to 7.5x
Correct Answer: B) 3-bet to 7.5x
AQs is a mandatory 3-bet against UTG opens from the Hijack. It has good equity against UTG's opening range and we want to isolate the original raiser.

Scenario 2: 77 vs BTN Open

Hero holds 77 in SB. BTN opens 2.5x.

A) Fold
B) 3-bet to 11x
Correct Answer: B) 3-bet to 11x
77 has enough equity to 3-bet for value against BTN's opening range. We want to deny equity to weaker hands and play a bigger pot with our pair.

Scenario 3: KQs vs 3-bet

Hero opens KQs in CO, SB 3-bets.

A) Fold
B) Call
C) 4-bet
Correct Answer: B) Call
KQs has sufficient equity to call against SB's 3-betting range. It flops well and has good playability post-flop.

Scenario 4: KJo vs UTG

Hero holds KJo in BB vs UTG open.

A) Fold
B) Call
C) 3-bet
Correct Answer: A) Fold
KJo is too weak to continue against UTG's strong opening range. The reverse implied odds make this a clear fold.

Scenario 5: 99 vs 3-bet

Hero opens 99 in CO, SB 3-bets.

A) Fold
B) Call
Correct Answer: B) Call
99 has enough equity to continue against SB's 3-betting range. Folding would be too tight here given the price we're getting.

Scenario 6: ATo vs BTN

Hero holds ATo in BB vs BTN open.

A) Fold
B) Call
Correct Answer: B) Call
ATo is strong enough to defend against BTN's wide opening range. We have sufficient equity to continue in position.

Scenario 7: TT vs 3-bet

Hero opens TT in HJ, CO 3-bets.

A) Fold
B) Call
Correct Answer: B) Call
TT is too strong to fold against a CO 3-bet. We have good equity against their value range and potential fold equity.

Scenario 8: AKs vs 5-bet

4-bet pot, facing 5-bet all-in with AKs.

A) Fold
B) Call
Correct Answer: B) Call
AKs has sufficient equity against QQ+/AK range. We're getting the right price to call all-in preflop.

Scenario 9: 65s vs UTG

Hero holds 65s in BB vs UTG open.

A) Fold
B) Call
Correct Answer: A) Fold
65s is too weak against UTG's strong range. The suitedness doesn't compensate for the positional disadvantage.

Scenario 10: QJs vs BTN

Hero holds QJs in BB vs BTN open.

A) Fold
B) Call
Correct Answer: B) Call
QJs has excellent playability postflop with its straight and flush potential. Good equity to defend against BTN's wide range.
