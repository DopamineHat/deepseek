Complete Poker Preflop Quiz

Scenario 1: AQs vs UTG Raise

Hero holds AQs in HJ. UTG opens 2.5bb.

A) Fold
B) 3-bet to 7.5bb
Correct Answer: B) 3-bet to 7.5bb
Bet Sizing Logic: 3bb open + 1bb per caller (UTG open 2.5bb → 7.5bb total)
Range Alignment: HJ 3-bet vs UTG (7.5bb): AQs+, AK, JJ+, A5s-A2s

Scenario 2: 77 vs BTN Open

Hero holds 77 in SB. BTN opens 2.5bb.

A) Fold
B) 3-bet to 11bb
Correct Answer: B) 3-bet to 11bb
Bet Sizing Logic: SB 3-bet vs BTN (4x open + 1bb) → 4×2.5bb + 1bb = 11bb
SB 3-bet vs BTN (11bb): 77+, AQo+, KJs+, 54s-98s
