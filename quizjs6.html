Complete Poker Preflop Quiz

Scenario 1: AQs vs UTG Raise

Hero holds AQs in HJ. UTG opens 2.5x.

A) Fold
B) 3-bet to 7.5x
Correct Answer: B) 3-bet to 7.5x
Bet Sizing Logic: 3-bet to 3x open + 1x per caller (UTG open 2.5x → 7.5x total)
Range Alignment: HJ 3-bet vs UTG (7.5x): AQs+, AK, JJ+, A5s-A2s

Scenario 2: 77 vs BTN Open

Hero holds 77 in SB. BTN opens 2.5x.

A) Fold
B) 3-bet to 11x
Correct Answer: B) 3-bet to 11x
Bet Sizing Logic: SB 3-bet vs BTN (4x open + 1x) → 4x2.5 + 1x = 11x
SB 3-bet vs BTN (11x): 77+, AQo+, KJs+, 54s-98s

Scenario 3: KQs vs 3-bet

Hero opens 2.5x in CO. SB 3-bets to 11x.

A) Fold
B) Call 11x
C) 4-bet to 25x

CO vs SB 3-bet (11x) calls: JJ-TT, AQs-AJs, KQs, 76s-98s
